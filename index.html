Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Wiserflo handles information when you use the app. Wiserflo is designed as a local-first budgeting app. In the current version, your financial records are mainly stored on your device, and optional cloud access only happens if you choose to use Google Drive backup or restore.

Wiserflo is built to help people manage real financial pressure with clarity and care. That includes being direct about what the app stores, what it sends off-device, and what remains fully under your control.

1.Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to the Wiserflo app operated by Wiserflo Labs. Wiserflo helps users track bills, debts, income, savings, and spending in a practical, lower-stress way.

In its current version, Wiserflo does not run a separate user account backend for core budgeting features. Most information you enter is stored locally on your own device.

2.Information We Collect

Wiserflo may handle the following categories of information:

Information you enter

Bills, due dates, debt records, income entries, savings goals, spend logs, and related planning information you choose to add.

App settings

Preferences such as reminder settings, budget settings, backup preferences, and local app state.

Google account details

If you connect Google Drive backup, Wiserflo may access your Google account email, ID, and display name to complete backup and restore.

Backup files

If you export or back up data, the backup may include your budgeting records and settings needed to restore the app later.

3.How We Use Information

  • To store and display the budgeting information you choose to enter.
  • To calculate budgeting summaries, monthly allocations, safe-to-spend values, reminders, and planning suggestions.
  • To create exports and backups when you request them.
  • To restore your information when you choose to import or restore a backup.
  • To operate optional Google Drive backup and restore.
  • To respond if you contact us for support, feedback, or privacy questions.

Wiserflo does not currently use your financial entries for advertising, data brokerage, or third-party marketing profiling.

4.Google Drive Backup

Wiserflo offers an optional Google Drive backup feature. If you choose to use it, the app connects to your Google account for the limited purpose of saving or restoring your Wiserflo backup file.

  • Google Drive backup is optional.
  • Wiserflo may access your Google account email, account ID, and display name when you connect this feature.
  • Backup files may contain your local Wiserflo data, including financial records and app settings needed for restore.
  • The backup is stored in your Google Drive app data area.
  • Google’s own privacy and security practices also apply to data stored in your Google account.

5.Notifications

If you allow notifications, Wiserflo may send reminders such as bill reminders, debt reminders, or other planning notifications based on your settings.

  • You can change or disable reminders in the app.
  • You can also manage notification permission in Android settings.
  • If notification previews appear on your lock screen, other people with access to your device may be able to see them.

6.Sharing And Disclosure

Wiserflo does not sell your personal data. We only share information in limited situations:

  • When you choose to export, share, import, back up, or restore your data.
  • When you choose to use Google Drive backup.
  • If required by law, legal process, or a valid government request.
  • If reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, or prevent abuse or fraud.

7.Data Retention

Since Wiserflo is local-first, your app data generally remains on your device until you edit it, remove it, uninstall the app, or overwrite it through import or restore.

  • Local budgeting data stays on your device unless you remove it.
  • Exported files stay wherever you save or share them.
  • Google Drive backups remain in your Google account until deleted or replaced.
  • Support emails or bug reports may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond and maintain support records.

8.Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information handled by the app, but no storage system, transmission method, or third-party cloud service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

  • Wiserflo’s core records are stored locally on your device.
  • Your phone lock, OS updates, and account protections are an important part of your security.
  • If you use Google Drive backup, the protection of that copy also depends on the security of your Google account and device.

9.Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app or uninstall it.
  • You can edit or delete entries inside Wiserflo.
  • You can turn reminders on or off.
  • You can choose whether to use Google Drive backup.
  • You can switch Google accounts for backup if you want to use a different account.
  • You can delete exported files or backups you created.

10.Children’s Privacy

Wiserflo is not directed to children under 13 as a primary audience. If you believe a child has provided personal information in a way that requires review or deletion, contact us and we will review the request.

11.Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Wiserflo changes. If we make material changes, we will update the date on this page and may also provide notice in the app when appropriate.
