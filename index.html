Local-First By Design

Your money data stays with you first.

Wiserflo is built to help people manage real financial pressure without shame. The current version of the app stores your budgeting data on your device, can send reminders if you turn them on, and only connects to Google Drive if you choose backup or restore.

On Device

Cloud Access

Optional

Selling Data

Never

Current Version

No backend

Wiserflo does not currently run its own cloud account system, sync server, or advertising stack.

Optional Connection

Google Drive

Used only for backup and restore if you choose to connect it.

You can keep using Wiserflo without connecting Google Drive. Core budgeting features work without creating an account.

Section 1

Who We Are

This Privacy Policy explains how Wiserflo Labs operates the Wiserflo app and how information is handled when you use it. Wiserflo is a budgeting and financial-planning app designed to help people track bills, debts, income, savings, and spending in a calmer and more practical way.

In the current version, Wiserflo is primarily a local-first app. That means your core financial records are stored on your device unless you choose to export them or back them up using an optional feature such as Google Drive backup.

Section 2

Information We Collect

We collect and handle information in the following categories:

Information You Enter

Bills, due dates, debt records, income entries, savings goals, spending logs, and other financial planning data you choose to add inside Wiserflo.

Device-Stored App Data

App settings, reminder preferences, budget calculations, notification inbox entries, and backup preferences stored on your device.

Google Account Details

If you connect Google Drive backup, Wiserflo may access your Google account email, account ID, and display name to complete backup and restore.

Backup Files

If you use Google Drive backup, a backup copy of your local database may be stored in your Google Drive app data area.

No ads, no sale of data. Wiserflo does not currently include third-party advertising SDKs and does not sell your personal information.

Section 3

How We Use Information

We use information only to operate and improve Wiserflo’s features, including to:

  • store and display your budgeting, debt, savings, and spending information inside the app;
  • calculate budgeting summaries, monthly allocation, safe-to-spend amounts, and other planning views;
  • schedule reminders and notifications if you turn them on;
  • create backup exports or restore data when you request those actions;
  • connect to your Google Drive account if you choose backup or restore through Google Drive; and
  • respond to support, bug, or privacy requests if you contact us.

We do not use your budgeting data to profile you for ads, sell it to data brokers, or build a separate marketing database from your financial records.

Section 4

Google Drive Backup

Wiserflo offers an optional Google Drive backup and restore feature. If you choose to use it, the app requests access to your Google account for the limited purpose of saving and restoring your backup in your Google Drive app data folder.

  • Your Google account is connected only when you choose to use the Google Drive backup feature.
  • Wiserflo may access your Google account email, account ID, and display name to identify the connected account.
  • Your backup file may contain the data you entered into Wiserflo, including financial entries and app settings required for restore.
  • The backup is stored in Google Drive’s app data area, which is generally hidden from the normal Drive file list.
  • Google’s handling of data in your Google account is also subject to Google’s own privacy terms and policies.
Important: If you export or back up your data to a third-party service, that copy is no longer only on your device. The privacy and security of that copy also depend on the service you chose to use.

Section 5

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Wiserflo can send reminders such as bill reminders, debt reminders, or other planning notifications. Notification content may appear on your lock screen or in your device’s notification tray depending on your phone settings.

  • You can disable or change reminder settings inside the app.
  • You can also manage notification permission in your Android system settings.
  • If notifications are visible on your lock screen, people with access to your device may be able to see reminder content.

Section 6

Sharing And Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your financial data except in the following limited situations:

  • At your direction: when you choose to export, share, back up, or restore data.
  • Service providers you choose to use: such as Google Drive for optional backup and restore.
  • Legal reasons: if required by law, regulation, legal process, or valid government request.
  • Protection and safety: when reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, or prevent fraud or abuse.

If you contact us by email for support, we will receive the information you send in that message and any attachments you choose to include.

Section 7

Data Retention

Because Wiserflo is local-first, your app data generally stays on your device until you edit it, delete it, uninstall the app, or overwrite it through an import or restore.

  • Local data remains on your device unless removed by you or deleted as part of app/device removal.
  • If you create backups, those backups remain where you saved them until you remove them.
  • If you back up to Google Drive, the backup can remain in your Google account until it is replaced or deleted.
  • Support emails or bug reports may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond and keep basic support records.

Section 8

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information handled by the app, but no method of electronic storage, transmission, or third-party cloud service is completely secure.

  • Core app data is stored locally on your device.
  • Your device security settings, lock screen security, backups, and account protections also matter.
  • If you use cloud backup, the security of that copy also depends on the security of your Google account and device.

You should use a device passcode, keep your OS updated, and protect the Google account you use for backups with strong security settings.

Section 9

Your Choices

You can make the following choices at any time:

  • stop using the app or uninstall it;
  • edit or delete entries inside Wiserflo;
  • turn reminders and notifications on or off;
  • choose whether to connect Google Drive backup;
  • change Google Drive accounts if you want to back up to a different account; and
  • delete exported files or backups you created.

If you want to ask a privacy question or request help, contact us at privacy@wiserflo.app.

Section 10

Children’s Privacy

Wiserflo is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly design the app for children’s use as a primary audience. If you believe a child has provided personal information through Wiserflo in a way that requires review or deletion, contact us and we will review the request.

Section 11

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as Wiserflo changes. If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page and may also provide notice inside the app when appropriate.

Your continued use of Wiserflo after an updated Privacy Policy becomes effective means the updated policy applies going forward.
